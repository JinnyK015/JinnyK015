Research EQ and IQ

What is Emotional Intelligence?

Emotional intelligence is ability to understand and manage their own emotions, and to understand and positively influence the emotions of others.

How is it different to IQ?

IQ is logical reasoning, word comprehension and math skills but EQ is partially determined by how a person relates to others and maintains emotional control.

Why is it important?

Emotional intelligence has been linked to job success and more satisfying relationships in life. Traditionally people thought IQ was important for work, but now we know both intelligences will effect job performance, relationships, and overall well-being.
