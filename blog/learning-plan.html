Learning-plan

What is your long-term goal and/or career pathway?

I don’t have clear goal for now. Because I just started learning coding. And at least I know there is plenty of variety in the IT field. So, I’m not sure which part is interesting to me. But for now, I’m learning the front-end part. So, I want to be a front-end developer first.

A description of your own strengths and limitations in relation to learning

My strengths are I am hardworking and honest about my limitations. So, I don’t hesitate to ask to help. But my limitation is I am too harsh with myself and can get quite stressed. I have good resilience so I will try to do best but sometimes if I’m struggling. I might want to give up, but being in the class will help me to keep going.

What skills (non-technical - human skills) would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

I really want to know how to cooperate in Tech field. I think it would be similar to other workplaces. But It’s hard to imagine how to cooperate in English, and with coding.

A commitment to how you will manage your workload in this programme.

First I will check why I have to do a task to understand the goal and purpose of the work. Second, I will make a plan for the most important parts. If I get stuck and can’t find my way I will ask for help on slack and my friends who work as developers. If I still couldn’t figure it out I will ask a facilitator.

A description of what you expect from the facilitation team

I’m not sure. It’s my first time studying in this style. I thought they would teach us and check all our homework or quizzes. But I know it will not work like that. It’s interesting and helpful to learn how to become a developer.

Any scheduling information such as block-out times when you are committed to other things.

I have scheduled my time to focus on studying for devAcademy, so I think I have plenty of time. I don’t have any major commitments.
